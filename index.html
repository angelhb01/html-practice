Mars:

From the solar system, Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun. This planet is also known as the Red Planet because of its red appearance. The land of mars looks nothing like Earth. Instead, it has a more dessert look rather than a more greenish look similar to Earth.

Facts About Mars:

Here are some interesting facts about Mars!

  • Has two small moons
  • About half the size of the Earth
  • The temperature is mostly cold with temperatures ranging from -85 to 240 degrees fahrenheit
  • A year in Mars is about 687 Earth days
